## About Tiny Systems

Tiny Systems research group has been established by [Kasım Sinan Yıldırım](https://sinanyil81.github.io/) since 2018. Our group does research on hardware and software for tiny embedded systems that have extreme resource and energy constraints. We are interested in problems at the intersection of embedded systems, internet of things (IoT), wireless communication and network protocols, self-organizing sensor networks, distributed algorithms, operating systems/run-times and many more.
